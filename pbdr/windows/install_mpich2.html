Install MPICH2
  1. Download both "mpich2-1.4.1p1-win-i32.msi " and "mpich2-1.4.1p1-win-x86-64.msi " from Argonne National Laboratory.


  2. Click on the saved files to start installation. The installation requires "Administrator" privilege.


  3. Click on the Next button.


  4. Choose "I Agree" to accept license, then click on the Next button.


  5. Click on the Next button.


  6. By default, 32bits of MPICH2 is installed at "C:\Program Files (x86)\MPICH2\" and 64bits is installled at "C:\Program Files\MPICH2\". I restrict the MPICH2 are used by myself, you may choose "Everyone" if this is a shared computer. Click on the Next button.


  7. Click on the Next button.


  8. Click on the Close button. You may need to check Windows Update.
