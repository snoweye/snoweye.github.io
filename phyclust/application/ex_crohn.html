Crohn's Disease -- An application for SNP data.

The R script (ex_crohn.r) demonstrates the settings for SNP data set, runs the phyclust() by two clusters, calculates distances, builds a neighbor-joining tree, and applies the plotnj() to draw the tree by the HAMMING distance. The data set has 1102 SNP sequences in 8 loci, and 39 sequences are unique. In the following,

  • The data set is available in phyclust and original from Hugot, J.P., et al. (2001).
  • The left plot draws the leaf edges in a standard scale, and clusters are showed in colors but only the unique sequences are clear.
  • The right plot draws the leaf edges in a larger scale, and the duplicated sequences are condensed to the unique sequences.
